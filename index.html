About Me

I am an Italian guy born in Desenzano del Garda and I have been living in Essen for 4 years.

After studying English and German both at the highschool of my hometown and at the university, I got my Bachelor Degree at the University of Trento in Italy and I had my first job experiences as Business Travel Agent in Bolzano (Südtirol) and as Marketing Assistant in Trento.

I decided then to move to Essen and I worked for 2 years as Customer Care Specialist for an e-Commerce of lamps in Düsseldorf.

In the meanwhile my passion for coding began and I decided later to attend an Umschulung as "Fachinformatiker Anwendungsentwickler", where I had the opportunity to learn a lot of programming languages and to work on concrete projects both alone and in team.

As your collaborator, I can offer you my willingness to grow together, to put myself out there and my ability to work in teams.
